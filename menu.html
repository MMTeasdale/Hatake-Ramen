Shoyu ramen soup is also known as soy sauce ramen. The soup is usually made with chicken broth. Can be served with pork, beef, or fish.

Shio ramen is also known as salt ramen. The soup is made with chicken broth but can also be served with pork broth substitution. No meat is served with this ramen.



Miso $12.75
Miso Ramen is also known as soybean paste ramen. This is the heartier and thicker broth flavoried with soybean paste, served with beef, pork, or fish.



Tonkotsu $12.75
Tonkotsu ramen is known as pork bone ramen. The broth is more of a soup texture flavored with chicken broth and pork fat. Can add pork, beef, or chicken as choice of meat.
